# SKILL: doggie_banner_factory
 
## Objective
 
Automatically generate complete banner campaigns for Doggie's Shopify store.
 
This skill acts as a **creative marketing studio**, producing:
 
- campaign concept
- banner copy
- AI image generation prompts
- layout guidance
- desktop and mobile versions
- A/B test variations
 
The goal is to create **high-converting Shopify hero banners** consistent with Doggie's visual identity.
 
---
 
# Brand Context
 
Doggie is a premium pet shop located in Lisbon.
 
Target audience:
 
Pet owners who:
 
- treat their pets as family
- value quality products
- appreciate premium experiences
- are willing to invest in their pet's well-being
 
Communication must feel:
 
- warm
- trustworthy
- joyful
- emotionally connected to pets
 
---
